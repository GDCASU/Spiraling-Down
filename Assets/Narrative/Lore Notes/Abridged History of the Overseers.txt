The Overseers, as they now call themselves, were first established around 1142 A.D. by a small group of alchemists and artisans seeking to create an artifact that could cure all disease and extend life. Information about this era is scarce, but as far as I can tell, during this fledgling period the organization was a guild known as the [redacted]. The founders shared their knowledge and conducted their experiments of transmutation in secret, but eventually rumors spread and ended up all the way in the ears of King [redacted]. Once the King's men investigated, fear of losing all their research spread among them, but they were offered a deal. The King would provide them funding and equipment for their goal, but once the artifact was created, it would become the sole property of the King. The alternative was execution, so they obviously had little choice.
